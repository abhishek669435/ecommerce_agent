Refund Policy:

- Customers can request a refund within 7 days of delivery.
- The item must be unused and in original condition.

Exceptions:
- Perishable items (food, flowers) are not refundable after delivery.
- Opened products cannot be refunded.

Special Cases:
- If item is damaged during delivery, refund is allowed.
- If wrong item is delivered, full refund is allowed.
